Visual Elements

This document showcases the enhanced visual design of the Terminus system:

  • Radial gradient background with subtle amber overlays
  • Terminal amber color scheme with bronze accents

Design Principles

The enhanced look emphasizes professional aesthetics while maintaining the terminal-inspired color palette. The design uses subtle gradients, shadows, and glowing effects to create depth and visual interest without being overwhelming.
